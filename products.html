Our extensive product range includes

Insulation Solutions

We offer an extensive range of Insulation products including ceramic blankets, mineral wool, fiberglass, Cellular glass, PIR & elastomeric foam insulation with ABS / DNV / LR class approvals. We also provide insulation consumables like insulation pins, lacing anchors, hog rings, knit mesh etc in stainless steel, galvanized steel & bimetallic versions.

Flooring Solutions

We offer a wide selection of flooring options suitable for marine applications, including non-slip and anti-fatigue flooring, carpeting, vinyl, and specialized marine coatings.

Wall and Ceiling Systems

Choose from our range of wall paneling, ceiling tiles, acoustic solutions, and fire-rated materials to create comfortable and aesthetically pleasing accommodation spaces.

Furniture and Fixtures

Discover our collection of marine-grade furniture and fixtures, including beds, seating, tables, storage solutions, lighting, and accessories. Our products combine functionality, durability, and ergonomic design.

Sanitary Systems

We provide a comprehensive range of marine sanitary systems, including toilets, sinks, showers, faucets, and piping, ensuring optimal hygiene and functionality on board.

HVAC Systems

We offer HVAC solutions designed specifically for marine environments, including air conditioning systems, ventilation equipment, and ducting components.

Class Rated Doors & Shutters

We offer class rated marine internal & external doors including watertight doors, A60 / A0 / B15 Doors (internal & external) A60 rolling shutters etc.

Safety Equipment

Enhance onboard safety with our selection of safety equipment, including fire extinguishers, life rafts, emergency lighting, alarms, and personal protective equipment (PPE).
