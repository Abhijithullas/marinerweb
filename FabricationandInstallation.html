Carpentry & Flooring

At MARINER, we offer comprehensive carpentry and flooring solutions specifically tailored for marine accommodation spaces. With our expertise in marine outfitting, we provide high-quality craftsmanship and durable materials to create functional, aesthetically pleasing, and safe environments for maritime living and working areas.

Carpentry Services:
  • Paneling and Finishing: We provide expert paneling and finishing services, ensuring smooth and visually appealing surfaces. Our carpenters work with a variety of materials, including marine-grade plywood, veneers, laminates, and solid wood, to create elegant and long-lasting finishes.
  • Custom Cabinetry: We specialize in designing and crafting custom cabinetry solutions that maximize storage space and enhance the overall functionality of marine accommodation areas. Our skilled carpenters meticulously construct cabinets, closets, and other storage units that meet the unique requirements of vessels and offshore platforms.
  • Furniture Fabrication: Our team of experienced carpenters can fabricate custom furniture pieces to optimize space utilization and ensure a cohesive design throughout the accommodation spaces. From beds and desks to seating and tables, we craft furniture that combines comfort, durability, and style.
Flooring Solutions:

  • Expert Installation: Our skilled craftsmen have extensive experience in marine flooring installation. They follow industry best practices, ensuring precision, durability, and compliance with safety regulations. We work efficiently to minimize downtime and deliver outstanding results.

Insulation

At MARINER, we specialize in providing top-quality marine insulation solutions for a wide range of marine vessels and offshore structures. Our experienced team of insulation experts utilizes industry-leading materials and techniques to ensure optimal thermal and acoustic performance, energy efficiency, and safety in marine environments.

Our Marine Insulation Services :
  • Fire Protection Insulation: Fire safety is of paramount importance in marine environments. Our fire protection insulation solutions ensure compliance with stringent fire safety regulations and provide essential protection for the vessel and its occupants. We install fireresistant insulation materials that delay the spread of flames and minimize the risk of fire-related incidents.
  • Thermal Insulation: We offer comprehensive thermal insulation solutions to regulate and control heat transfer within marine accommodation spaces. Our insulation materials effectively reduce heat gain or loss, creating comfortable and energy-efficient environments. We carefully select insulation materials that are suitable for marine applications and meet the specific requirements of each project.
  • Acoustic Insulation: Noise control is a critical consideration in marine environments, where noise from machinery, engines, and other sources can affect occupant comfort and safety. Our acoustic insulation solutions effectively reduce noise transmission, creating quieter and more pleasant living and working spaces. We utilize high-performance acoustic insulation materials and techniques to achieve optimal noise reduction.
  • Condensation Control: Condensation can be a significant concern in marine environments due to temperature variations and humidity. Our pipe insulation solutions effectively control condensation, preventing moisture buildup and protecting the integrity of the piping systems. This helps prevent corrosion, mold growth, and potential damage to surrounding structures.
  • HVAC Insulation: We provide insulation solutions for HVAC systems, including ductwork, piping, and equipment. Our insulation materials effectively minimize heat transfer, reduce energy loss, and optimize the performance of the HVAC system. We ensure that the insulation is properly installed to maximize energy efficiency and maintain comfortable temperatures within the marine accommodation areas.
  • Cryogenic Insulation: We offer advanced insulation solutions for pipes and equipment that handle cryogenic fluids, such as liquefied natural gas (LNG) or liquid nitrogen. Our insulation materials are specifically designed to minimize heat transfer, ensuring the safe and efficient storage and transportation of cryogenic substances. We pay meticulous attention to insulation thickness, vapor barriers, and sealing to prevent thermal losses and maintain the desired low temperatures.
  • High-Temperature Insulation: For pipes and equipment operating at high temperatures, we provide effective insulation solutions that reduce heat transfer and protect surrounding structures and personnel. Our high-temperature insulation materials are capable of withstanding extreme temperatures while maintaining excellent thermal resistance. We carefully select insulation materials that are suitable for marine applications and can endure the demanding conditions of high-temperature environments

HVAC & Machinery Space Ventilation

At MARINER, we specialize in providing comprehensive HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) and machinery space ventilation solutions for marine vessels and offshore structures. Our experienced team of engineers and technicians is dedicated to creating comfortable and safe environments in marine accommodation areas and ensuring efficient ventilation for machinery spaces.

HVAC Services:
  • Design and Installation: We offer HVAC system design and installation services tailored to meet the specific requirements of marine accommodation areas. Our team utilizes advanced design software and considers factors such as space limitations, occupancy levels, and environmental conditions to create HVAC systems that provide optimal comfort, temperature control, and energy efficiency.
  • Air Conditioning: We provide reliable air conditioning solutions for marine accommodation spaces, ensuring consistent and comfortable temperatures even in challenging maritime conditions. Our expertise includes selecting appropriate cooling systems, ductwork design, and efficient air distribution to create pleasant living and working environments onboard vessels and offshore structures.
  • Heating Systems: We design and install heating systems that provide effective temperature control and ensure comfort during colder periods. Our heating solutions are tailored to the specific needs of the marine environment, taking into account energy efficiency and safety requirements.
Machinery Space Ventilation Services:
  • Ventilation System Design: We specialize in designing ventilation systems for machinery spaces, ensuring proper airflow, temperature control, and effective removal of exhaust gases, fumes, and heat generated by onboard machinery. Our team considers the unique requirements of each machinery space, including air change rates, ductwork design, filtration, and fire safety regulations.
  • Exhaust System Installation: We install efficient exhaust systems to remove hazardous or unwanted gases from machinery spaces, ensuring the safety of personnel and equipment. Our expertise includes selecting appropriate exhaust fans, ductwork, and filtration systems to maintain air quality and prevent the accumulation of harmful substances.
  • Engine Room Ventilation: We design and install ventilation systems specifically for engine rooms, considering the high heat and exhaust emissions generated by marine engines. Our ventilation solutions promote airflow, control temperatures, and optimize engine performance while complying with relevant safety regulations.
