Our HVAC design process follows a systematic approach to deliver the best results

HVAC Design

At MARINER, we specialize in providing comprehensive HVAC design solutions tailored to the unique requirements of marine accommodation spaces. Our team of experienced engineers and designers are experts in creating efficient, reliable, and innovative HVAC systems that ensure optimal comfort and air quality in maritime environments. Customized HVAC Solutions:

We understand that each marine accommodation project has specific needs and challenges. Our HVAC design services are customized to meet the requirements of vessels, offshore platforms, and other maritime structures. We take into account factors such as space limitations, environmental conditions, regulatory compliance, and energy efficiency. Design Process:

Project Assessment

We conduct a thorough assessment of the marine accommodation area, taking into consideration the vessel's layout, intended use, occupancy requirements, and environmental conditions.

Load Calculation

Our engineers perform accurate heat load calculations to determine the heating and cooling requirements of the space, ensuring that the HVAC system is properly sized for maximum efficiency.

Equipment Selection

We carefully select HVAC equipment that is specifically designed for marine applications, taking into account factors such as durability, corrosion resistance, vibration tolerance, and energy efficiency

Air Distribution Design

Our team designs the air distribution system to ensure proper airflow, ventilation, and temperature control throughout the accommodation spaces. We consider factors such as occupant comfort, air quality, and compliance with industry standards

Control Systems

We incorporate advanced control systems to optimize HVAC performance, including temperature and humidity control, energy management, and integration with other building automation systems.

Noise Control

We implement noise control measures to minimize HVAC system noise, ensuring a comfortable and peaceful environment for occupants.

Compliance and Safety

We prioritize compliance with industry regulations and safety standards. Our HVAC designs adhere to relevant guidelines, including fire safety codes, ventilation requirements, and environmental regulations. We work closely with classification societies, regulatory bodies, and client specifications to ensure full compliance.

Expertise and Experience

With our extensive experience in marine accommodation outfitting, our team has successfully delivered HVAC solutions for a wide range of vessels and offshore structures. We stay up-to-date with the latest industry advancements and technologies, ensuring that our designs incorporate the most innovative and efficient HVAC systems available.

Architectural Design

Our architectural design process is collaborative and client-focused, ensuring that the final design aligns with project objectives and exceeds expectations:

Concept Development

We begin by understanding the client's vision, project requirements, and operational needs. Our team then develops creative concepts that balance form and function, considering factors such as interior layout, traffic flow, natural lighting, and ergonomics.

Detailed Design

Once the concept is approved, we move into the detailed design phase. Our architects create precise drawings, 3D models, and specifications that capture the design intent and guide the construction process. We pay meticulous attention to every detail, ensuring that the design seamlessly integrates with other outfitting elements.
